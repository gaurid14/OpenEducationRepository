About OER AgentFlow

OER AgentFlow was born from the mission to solve the single greatest challenge in Open Educational Resources (OER): **Trust and Quality Control**. While OER offers universal access, the sheer volume and lack of centralized review often lead to inconsistent quality, making adoption difficult for educators and students.

We leverage a network of **AI Agents** (powered by LangGraph and Gemini) to automatically review, score, and certify every piece of content submitted to our platform against predefined, objective rubrics. This ensures that every resource consumed by an engineering student is accurate, pedagogically sound, and up-to-date.

**Our goal is simple:** To be the most reliable, high-quality repository of engineering educational resources available online, using technology to ensure rapid scaling without compromising academic integrity.

Our Core Commitments

Integrity First

Every resource is evaluated by a transparent, rubric-based AI process, providing verifiable quality assurance.

Empowerment

We provide detailed feedback to contributors, empowering them to improve their material and grow the community.

Access & Scale

By automating the curation process, we can scale our library to meet the needs of all engineering streams globally.
